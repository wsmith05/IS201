Wakesurfing

  1. Why it's so fun

    • It's not very difficult
    • You can make up new tricks and techniques
    • You can do it with friends

  2. How to get into it

    • Find a friend that has a surf boat or wakeboat-just make sure it has an inboard motor
    • Watch tutorials or have someone teach you how to do it
    • If you like it, invest in a good board and even a boat!
    • Hyperlite sells some great boards that are also affordable. Click here for a collection of their boards.

  3. Difficulties

    • It's an expensive hobby if you buy your own boat
    • Some people take a while to get the hang of it
    • If you don't know what you're doing it can be dangerous
